About

Guide

No matter you are preparing for 112 GSAT, studying for the final exam one week away, or you just want to determine your working efficiency, Rolling Work is an ideal tool for you. Click 'Timer', you will see a clock and some buttons. With each click on 'roll' button, you will switch betweeen work and rest, and the app will record it. At the end of work, after you click save(login needed) button, you will get a record of work today. Moreover, you can see your history record on at your profile page. With this tool, hope you have a efficient day!

About Rolling Work

2022 spring is my last year at high school. Thus, I have a long summer this year. To fill the blank, I started to take cs50w online course to learn web development. Through this course, I learned a lot, and I couldn't to startup a project to sharpen my skills. At this moment, I saw a video made by my future senior brother in college whcih is about how he study. He used timer app in tablet and utilize 'lap time' function to record the length of time he breaks and works. I tried the method, too. But I found out that it's hard to recognized one period of time is rest or work. Then I decided to develop my own tool to help me study. Thus, Rolling Work was born!
